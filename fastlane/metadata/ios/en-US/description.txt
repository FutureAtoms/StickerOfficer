StickerOfficer makes it easy to create custom sticker packs using AI or your own photos.

CREATE WITH AI
Describe any sticker you want and AI generates it for you. From funny reactions to cute characters, the possibilities are endless.

BUILD PACKS
Organize stickers into packs of up to 30. Import from your photo library, use the editor to perfect them, or generate with AI.

SHARE VIA MESSAGING APPS
Share your sticker packs through the iOS share sheet to WhatsApp and other messaging apps.

COMMUNITY
Browse trending packs, discover creators, and join weekly creative challenges. Like and save your favorite packs.

POWERFUL EDITOR
Background removal, filters, text overlays, and bulk editing. Everything you need to create professional-looking stickers.
